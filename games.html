How to play:

• This game can be played either individually or with a group. Use the random number generator in the bottom left corner to generate a number between 0 and 10.
• If you get 0, skip a turn. If not, click the corresponding number on the number menu under the cards & answer the question honestly.
• Each answer corresponds to a particular score. Keep a tally of your score according to the list below (scroll down).
• After 3 rounds (or after each player has answered 3 questions), the player with the highest score is the 'red flag.'
